Install App

Download Gamepad for Android TV on both your Android phone and TV device.

2

Pair Devices

Enable Bluetooth and select your TV from the mobile app's device list.

3

Set Input

On your TV, select "Gamepad Input" in the Keyboard & Input settings.

4

Start Playing

Launch any game and enjoy console-like control from your phone.
